About Us

Fetch is a mobility and data service company that works with businesses in hospitality, education, and any campus based operations to enhance mobility for everyone.

We are committed to - Saving Energy - Enhancing Life - Increasing Productivity - Reducing Effort - Cutting Costs

With these parameters staying as our ethos, we are developing mobility products to transform lives, improve business operations and offer futuristic experiences to all around. We are exploring options to make micro mobility seamlessly accessible to all wherever possible.

We are also parallely building on our vision to develop a complete mobility ecosystem for people in whichever walk of life they exist and operate. Through this we believe our community of people could enhance seamless mobility across any place we have a presence.

Our Journey
& Milestones

Fetch Mobility began its journey with a simple goal: to make commutes within campuses easy and effortless. It started with a few handful of our e-trikes and has now grown, with its presence extending to many campuses and factories. Let’s take a look at the various breakthroughs we made in the past year….

OCTOBER 2023

+
-

In October 2023, our journey began with a clear mission: to transform micro-mobility and provide enhanced, accessible mobility solutions for all. We began by implementing a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) to understand how people would perceive and adapt to a new mobility solution within campus environments.

JANUARY 2024

+
-

By January 2024, we had developed the first iteration of our Classic Trike, and rigorous testing was underway. Our founders were dedicated to creating a completely indigenous product, with all technological capabilities designed and built in-house to ensure the highest standards of innovation and quality.

FEBRUARY 2024

+
-

In February 2024, we launched our pioneer fleet of Trikes and began conducting live demos at multiple campuses. It didn’t take long to grab the attention, as institutions began to realize the potential of integrating these cutting-edge vehicles into their campuses. A major breakthrough came when Century Group, one of Bangalore's most reputed real estate firms, recognized the opportunity and decided to future-proof their campuses with our products.

MARCH 2024

+
-

The following month, in March 2024, our flagship Voltron CT2310 Trikes were successfully deployed at Vidyashilp University, transforming campus commutes and significantly enhancing the institution’s brand appeal. The experience was universally loved by students, staff, and faculty alike, making it a major milestone for the company.

MAY 2024

+
-

By May 2024, our second fleet was deployed at the Igus campus in Germany. The leadership there saw the value of our products in terms of saving cost, time, and energy, and our products were quickly embraced as part of their commitment to lean operations and efficiency.

JULY 2024

+
-

In July 2024, as our vision of scaling and making enhanced mobility accessible to all began to take shape, we officially rebranded as Fetch Mobility. This new name reflected our ambition to create a comprehensive mobility ecosystem that serves diverse needs across different sectors.

OCTOBER 2024

+
-

Finally, by October 2024, we had refined our approach to scalability. The Classic Trike was upgraded, and we introduced our first Kick Scooter. Additionally, we developed an impressive software ecosystem and a custom Vehicle Access System, all built entirely in-house by our talented engineering team, setting the stage for the next phase of growth.
